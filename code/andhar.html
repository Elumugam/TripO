Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh lies along the southeastern coast of India, bordered by Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Odisha, and the Bay of Bengal. Its capital is Amaravati, while Visakhapatnam is the largest and most vibrant city.
