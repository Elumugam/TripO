Assam

Assam, in Northeast India, is a land of natural beauty and cultural heritage. From the sacred Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati to the wild Kaziranga National Park, it offers something for every traveler. Explore Majuli’s river island charm, Sivasagar’s royal monuments, and Haflong’s peaceful hills. Assam is where tea gardens, wildlife, and history come together to create a truly unique experience.
