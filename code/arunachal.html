Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, known as the "Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains," is a breathtakingly beautiful state located in the northeastern tip of India. It shares international borders with Bhutan, China, and Myanmar, and is famed for its pristine landscapes, diverse tribal cultures, and rich biodiversity.
